Undhiyu is a Gujarati mixed vegetable dish that is a regional specialty of Surat, Gujarat, India.

Dal Dhokli

Daal dhokli is an Indian dish common in Rajasthani and Gujarati cuisine, made by boiling wheat flour pieces in a pigeon pea stew.
